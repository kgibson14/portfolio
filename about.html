About me

About Me

Hey — I’m Krista Gibson. I studied Interactive Design at Kennesaw State University and work in UX, where I’m happiest untangling complex systems and turning them into experiences that feel effortless, intuitive, and accessible. I care deeply about inclusive design and building products that work for real people in real situations.

Outside of UX, I’m relentlessly curious and always chasing the next creative or adrenaline-fueled thing. I paint, build physical art, shoot photography (especially motorsports and live events), and spend a lot of time rock climbing. I love projects that live somewhere between structure and chaos — whether that’s designing a scalable interface or experimenting with a new medium.

Most of my free time is split between creating things, being outdoors, and going on little adventures with my dogs. I bring that same energy into my work: curious, hands-on, collaborative, and always pushing for something that feels a little more thoughtful than what existed before.
