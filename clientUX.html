Client UX & Website Design Work (NDA)

Designing conversion-focused digital experiences across industries and client needs

ROLE
UX Researcher & Designer
TIMELINE
Ongoing across multiple engagements(2023-2024)
TOOLS
Figma, Miro, usability best practices, analytics-informed design

Overview

As part of an agency team, I worked on a wide range of client engagements across multiple industries, contributing UX and UI design support for landing pages, marketing websites, and digital campaigns.

Projects ranged from quick-turn promotional pages to larger website redesigns, each tailored to specific business goals such as increasing traffic, improving engagement, supporting seasonal campaigns, or modernizing brand presence.

The Problem

Across clients, common challenges included:

  • Landing pages that were visually outdated or misaligned with brand direction
  • Pages that lacked clear hierarchy or calls to action
  • Seasonal or promotional content that needed to be launched quickly
  • Websites that no longer supported evolving business goals or user expectations

Each client required a solution that balanced brand expression, usability, and conversion ‚Äî often under tight timelines.

The Solution

I designed UX-driven landing pages and website experiences that:

  • Clearly communicated value propositions
  • Guided users toward primary actions
  • Highlighted seasonal or promotional content effectively
  • Reflected updated brand identities and messaging
  • Maintained accessibility and usability best practices

Solutions were tailored per client while leveraging consistent UX principles.

Contraints & Consideration

  • Tight deadlines, especially for seasonal and promotional campaigns
  • Varying brand maturity levels across clients
  • Need to balance creativity with established brand guidelines
  • Multiple stakeholders with different success metrics
  • Ensuring accessibility and usability across diverse audiences

These constraints required adaptable design approaches and efficient collaboration.

Research & Discovery

Discovery efforts varied by project scope, but often included:

  • Stakeholder and account team input on business goals
  • Review of existing site performance and user behavior
  • Competitive and industry benchmarking
  • Content and messaging alignment with creative strategy

Research was scoped appropriately to timeline and project size.

Key Insights

  • Clear hierarchy and focused CTAs are critical for conversion
  • Even small UX improvements can significantly impact engagement
  • Consistency across landing pages builds trust and recognition
  • Seasonal and promotional content benefits from simplified, direct user flows
  • Collaboration between UX and creative teams elevates outcomes

Design & Exploration

Design work included:

  • Landing page layouts optimized for conversion
  • Homepage and section redesigns for clarity and storytelling
  • Visual hierarchy and content structuring
  • Responsive design considerations across devices
  • Supporting assets such as email designs and campaign components

Designs were iterated quickly to meet deadlines while maintaining quality.

Development Collaboration

I collaborated closely with:

  • Creative teams on imagery, messaging, and brand expression
  • Developers to ensure feasibility and clean handoff
  • Account teams to align designs with client goals and feedback

This cross-functional approach ensured designs were both compelling and implementable.

Validation & Exploration

Validation methods included:

  • Internal reviews and creative critiques
  • Client feedback rounds
  • Post-launch observation of engagement and performance trends
  • Iterative refinement for ongoing campaigns

Reflection

This body of work strengthened my ability to adapt UX principles across industries and client needs. Designing within varied brand systems and timelines reinforced the importance of clarity, flexibility, and collaboration in agency environments.

If given more time per engagement, I would expand user testing and deeper analytics analysis to further quantify impact.
